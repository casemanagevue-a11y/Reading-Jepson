## Template Strategy

### Create These Template Sets

#### Full Weeks (5 days)
Create 20-25 templates for normal weeks:
- Days 1-5 fully planned
- All passages and activities

#### Short Weeks (4 days)
Create 8-10 templates for 4-day weeks:
- Days 1-4 only
- Condensed activities
- Skip Friday assessment OR move to Day 4

#### Very Short Weeks (3 days)
Create 4-5 templates for 3-day weeks:
- Days 1-3 only
- Essential content only
- Focus on vocabulary + one passage

## Assignment Workflow

### For Each Student (Per Quarter)

1. **Identify which weeks are short** based on calendar
2. **Assign appropriate templates:**
   - Regular weeks ‚Üí 5-day template
   - Holiday weeks ‚Üí 4-day template
   - Break weeks ‚Üí 3-day template or skip
3. **Result:** Each student has 7-9 assignments (some quarters have fewer due to breaks)

## Quick Start

1. **Go to Calendar Import** (`/teacher/calendar/import`)
2. **Paste all your calendar text** (the one you showed me)
3. **Click "Parse Calendar"** - system auto-detects ~30 events
4. **Review parsed events** - edit titles/types if needed
5. **Click "Import"** - all dates saved
6. **Create templates** - mix of 3-day, 4-day, and 5-day
7. **Assign to students** - system knows which weeks are short

The assignment system will show you the actual dates for each week, accounting for all your holidays!

---

## Pro Tips

- **Create 3/4/5-day versions** of the same content
- **Reuse templates** across quarters when appropriate
- **Short weeks** focus on essential content (vocab + one passage)
- **Calendar auto-calculates** which days students actually have class
- **Fridays are always minimum** - already built into your workflow
