# Navigation Guide - Week Template System

## Main Navigation Bar

After logging in as a teacher, you'll see these links in the top navigation:

- **Dashboard** - Overview and quick actions
- **Students** - Manage students & Google Classroom import
- **Templates** - View & create reusable week templates
- **Assignments** - Assign templates to students (9 per quarter)
- **Calendar** - Manage school calendar (days off, holidays)
- **Library** - Vocab, affixes, and passage library

### Step 2: Create Week Templates
ğŸ“ **Go to:** Templates (top nav) or Dashboard â†’ "Create Template"

**Create 9 templates** (one for each week of the quarter):
- Template Name: "Week 1 - Ancient Rome", "Week 2 - Roman Republic", etc.
- Grade Level: (optional) e.g., "6"
- Unit: (optional) e.g., "Ancient Civilizations"
- Subject: ELA, Science, or History
- Then add all content:
  - Day 1: Vocabulary & Affixes
  - Day 2: Weekly Passage & Literal Questions
  - Day 4: Main Idea Questions
  - Day 5: Friday Passage & Assessment

**Result:** You now have 9 reusable lesson plans

ğŸ“ **Go to:** Students (top nav) or Dashboard â†’ "Manage Students"

### Step 4: Assign Templates to Students
ğŸ“ **Go to:** Assignments (top nav) or Dashboard â†’ "Assign to Students"

**For each student:**
1. Select the student
2. Choose quarter (1-4)
3. Choose week number (1-9)
4. Select which template to assign
5. Click "Assign Template"

**Repeat 9 times** for each student (one for each week)

**Tip:** You can assign the same template to multiple students

---
